Soy Sauce, Salt, and Pepper Chicken

Description

This chicken and rice recipe is a perfect blend of sweet, savory, and spicy. By including flavors from both Korean cuisine (with a special soy-sauce glaze) and Chinese cuisine (with a special salt and pepper chicken seasoning), you'll be able to create a home-y dish that everyone will be sure to love!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • MSG
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Serrano Peppers
  • Thai Chili Peppers
  • Soy Sauce
  • Maggi Seasoning
  • Mirin
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Chili Flakes
  • Minced Garlic
  • Green Onions
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce
    • Mix Together:
      • 1 Cup of Water
      • 1/4 Cup of Soy Sauce
      • 1/4 Cup of Maggi Seasoning
      • 1 TBSP of Mirin
      • 1 TBSP of Sesame Oil
      • 1 TBSP of MSG
      • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
      • 1 TBSP of Minced Garlic
      • Chili Flakes - As Much as You Want
      • Sesame Seeds - As Much as You Want
      • Green Onions - As Much as You Want
  2. Season the Chicken
    • Use Your Preferred Seasonings
    • Mine Are:
      • Salt
      • Chili Powder
      • Garlic Powder
      • Onion Powder
  3. Cook the Chicken
    • Air Fry @ 400 for 24-30 minutes - skin side down, flip halfway
  4. Make Salt and Pepper Seasoning
    • Mix Together:
      • 1 TSP Sugar
      • 1/2 TSP Salt
      • 1/4 TSP Pepper
  5. Infuse Chicken with Seasoning and Peppers
    • Once the chicken is done cooking:
      1. Heat up a pan with oil, and toss in diced serrano peppers
      2. Season the peppers with the salt/pepper seasoning, toss, and cook till slightly charred
      3. Toss in the chicken with the peppers, season the chicken with extra salt/pepper seasoning
  6. Serve the chicken over rice, and add sauce as preferred
